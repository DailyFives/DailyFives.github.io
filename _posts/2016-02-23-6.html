2. How Google Ventures Uses Design Sprints to Ship Great Products - by Matt McCue Share on: | |

Matt McCue starts the post with the famous quote from former Netscape CEO, Jim Barksdale:

If we have data, let's look at data. If all we have are opinions, let's go with mine.

The design sprint works like this:

Over the course of five days, sprint participants map out a project goal and find its problems (Monday), brainstorm solutions (Tuesday), make progress decisions (Wednesday), prototype (Thursday), and have users experience the prototype (Friday).

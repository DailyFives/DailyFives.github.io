2. Stress-Free Productivity: How to Make To-do Lists Work For You - by Shayla PriceShare on: | |

In this post, Shayla Price explains how to make To-Do lists work. What might start as a blank page will end up with multiple pending tasks when maintaining todo lists and it can be very daunting. Shayla first tells you what is the problem with todo lists and gives you lot of practical advice to make it work for you.

The problems with to-do lists:
  • First task feels most urgent
  • Every task looks the same
  • Rescheduling tasks is tough
  • There's always something else to do
